At-the-door script

Five steps. Quiet confidence beats a hard pitch every time.

Hand them the phone

Open to the live site. Don't pitch — let the page do it. Say: "Hi, I'm Isaac and this is my brother Elijah. We live a couple streets over. Our site explains what we do — mind taking a look?"

Wait quietly

Let them scroll. The hero, price chips, and portrait do the work in about fifteen seconds. Resist the urge to fill the silence.

If they say yes

Ask: car, date, time. Send the "booking confirmation" SMS above so they have a record of what they agreed to.

If they say maybe

Hand them the printed flyer. Say: "No worries — here's a little flyer with our number and a QR code. Text us whenever." Then move on.

If they say no

Thank them politely and move to the next door. Never argue, never linger. A real "no" is a gift — it frees up your time for the next "yes."
